ABOUT US


Venture vibe is the firm started by the members of entrepreneur students of class 11 2023-24. This firm is started under the guidance of Mr. MANOJ KUMAR JAIN This is the small initiative taken to teach the students about business. An entrepreneur is an individual who creates a new business, bearing most of the risks and enjoying most of the rewards. This is the first session of Entrepreneurship that has been started in the school. This small step taken by the KRISHNA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL will not only teach but will also encourage and motivate the students to do something innovative and creative. This will inculcate the habit of self earning the students. The first session of entrprepreneurship subject 2023-24 will leave alegacy behind for the upcoming sessions of our school. The VENTURE VIBE is not only a grade based project but it teaches the value of money and give lessons about real life earnings. It will be an honour for students if not only teachers but parents also come and become a part of this young entrepreneurial world, where some life changing experiments and products can solve real life probl ems and the business attitude of young entrepreneurs will blow their minds away.
